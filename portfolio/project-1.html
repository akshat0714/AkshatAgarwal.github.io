Project 01 · Founder

Neorons — personalized learning, powered by AI.

A platform that generates personalized learning content for students globally, built so that learners who are usually left behind — including special-needs students — get materials shaped to how they actually learn.

RoleFounder
FocusGenerative AI personalization
Reach3,000+ students · worldwide
Linkneorons.ai

The problem

Personalization that scales — and includes everyone.

Most learning content is one-size-fits-all. For students with different needs and abilities, that gap is even wider — and the tools to close it rarely reach the classrooms that need them most.

Neorons uses generative AI to author and adapt learning materials to each student, combining generative personalization, agentic orchestration, and knowledge-graph reasoning so content stays accurate while flexing to the learner. As founder, I led product and deployment, partnering directly with educators and schools to put it in front of real students.

What I built

  • Generative content authoring
  • Per-learner personalization
  • Agentic orchestration
  • Knowledge-graph reasoning
  • School deployment
