About me

I build AI that personalizes learning — and reasons about the real world.

I'm Akshat Agarwal, a student researcher focused on generative and agentic AI, with hands-on experience in personalization systems, knowledge reasoning, and applied machine learning.

As the founder of Neorons, I've built a platform that generates personalized learning content for students globally, working with schools worldwide and supporting more than 3,000 special-needs students. Alongside that, I conduct applied research — turning generative AI into solutions for education and autonomous driving, and translating that work into published papers and patent filings.

I care about analytical rigor and attention to detail, but just as much about impact: technology that reaches people who are usually left out. Outside the lab I'm a competitive taekwondo athlete and a varsity basketball player, and I run a nonprofit that teaches the sport to students who rarely get the chance.
